Normal

This is a demo that will show you the difference between vertex and face normals.

Files:
Normals.cpp - Main file, contains WinMain and global Init, Main Loop, and Shutdown functions. In this case it also contains the normal calculation and display code.
normals.dsp/normals.dsw Microsoft Visual C++ 6.0 project files.

Controls:
N - Toggle between face and vertex normals
W - Turn wireframe on and off
D - Turn the display of normals on and off
